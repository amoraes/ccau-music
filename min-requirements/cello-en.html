If there is no musical instruction for this instrument in your local congregation it is highly advised to seek a professional music teacher for lessons, as self-learning a musical instrument can lead to misconceptions and bad habits that are harder to correct afterwards.
All the methods required are widely used in Australia to make it easier to find a music teacher that can help the student to reach the required standards.
